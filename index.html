FILTRATION

You can't just buy things for the label - it's ridiculous. We look our best in subdued colors, sophisticated cuts, and a general air of sleek understatement. I think God is the most fantastic designer. If you wear clothes that don't suit you, you're a fashion victim. You have to wear clothes that make you look better. Fashion is not something that exists in dresses only. Fashion is in the sky, in the street, fashion has to do with ideas, the way we live, what is happening.

RAPID TEST

When I was young, I lived like an old woman, and when I got old, I had to live like a young person. Sometimes incompetence is useful. It helps you keep an open mind. You can't just buy things for the label - it's ridiculous. Fashions fade, style is eternal. Comfort is very important to me. I think people live better in big houses and in big clothes.

WATER ANALYSIS

For me, art is about learning and about living with people. It's alive. Elegance isn't solely defined by what you wear. It's how you carry yourself, how you speak, what you read. Clothes mean nothing until someone lives in them. I remember walking the dog one day, I saw a car full of teenage girls, and one of them rolled down the window and yelled, 'Marc Jacobs!' in a French accent. Success isn't about the end result, it's about what you learn along the way.
